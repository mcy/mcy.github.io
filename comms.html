Art Commissions

I am a furry artist! In exchange for money, I’ll draw something for you. To explore the sort of work I do, check out my art gallery at art.mcyoung.xyz.

The best way to reach me for commissions is by whatever means you have to reach me: Twitter DMs, Telegram, Signal, or email me at mcyoung (at) mit (dot) edu.

The pricing below is sorted by style, and is generally negotiable based on the complexity of the subject. I can accept payment by PayPal and Venmo. I usually want to see 50% up-front for complex projects. Prices are in USD.

Notes

I will never draw hateful or illegal content of any kind, and I do not like drawing humans or in a realistic style. I will draw NSFW, kink, and gore, but I may not draw something if I do not feel I can do a good job.

I want to support furries in need: if you present proof of paying for another artist’s emergency commissions, I’ll discount my rates by that amount. Similarly, if you are a marginalized person without the means to pay my rate, am happy to provide discounted prices.

I retain copyright of my works; I do not do work for hire without a contract. By default, they are licensed to you, the commissioner, CC BY-NC-SA. This means you can share and modify the work, but you must always credit me and may not use it commercially.
